What is Cloud Kombucha?

Cloud Kombucha is a brand that endows Parisian consumers with fresh kombucha that is jam-packed with caffeine, probiotics and antioxidants. We are a sustainable brand that focuses on empowering the local community and promoting green tactics in the agricultural sector.
